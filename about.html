ABOUT THE BAND

MaerA is an alternative metal band based in Belgrade, Serbia. Although the band existed in different lineups for few years, with only the guitarist/lead vocalist and the founder of the band Tomislav Dumanovic remaining in the current lineup, the band truly formed in October of 2016 when Tomislav recruited drummer Vladan Markovic and the bassist Nebojsa Milosavljevic. After many played shows, band gained a loyal fanbase and an increadible feedback so they decided to record their first EP titled "Antfarm". They recruited Igor Canic as their new gitarist and won 2nd place on Klub Fest's Demo Fest together. They've begun to record their first demo album “Echo The Howl” that is available on Bandcamp, Soundcloud and Youtube right now. In april of 2019. the band announced that Igor and Nebojša are no longer a part of the band and that Nikola Tomic and Luka Bozinovic will be replacing them on guitar and bass. The band is currently working on their new material and is planning to head to the studio this summer. Their sound is defined by heavy riffs with arabian influence combined with brutally honest lyrics that tell tales of one's darkest moments but also criticize the society we live in and reveal it's true colours.
